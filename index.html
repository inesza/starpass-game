How to play?

As a jammer, your role is to avoid blockers

Use left and right arrow keys to avoid your opponents

1 overtaken opponent = 1 point

With a little help from your friends

Bump into your teammates for a little stamina boost

Fight them if you have to

If you bump into one of your opponents, you’ll have to fight your way past them. You can try either brute strength or ruse, but pay attention to your stats and theirs.

But don’t get too exhausted!

If you loose the fight, you’ll lose some stamina and must keep fighting.

If your stamina falls to 0, you’ll loose
